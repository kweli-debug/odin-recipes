Double Decker Tacos

Description

Soft taco on the outside, crispy taco on the inside. These are easier to eat than a regular taco. The soft taco keeps everything together.

Ingredients

  • 10 hard taco shells that are flat on the bottom called Stand n' Stuff
  • 10 small flour tortillas snack size
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 2 cups of chedder cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups of shredded lettuce
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans or 2 cups homeade refried beans
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef untill it browns
  2. Drain ground beef
  3. Add taco seasoing
  4. while beef is simmering put refried beans in a small sauce pan to warm over low heat. May need to add 1-2 tbs of water to the beans to thin them out a bit.
  5. Warm soft tortillas in a dry skillet over low to medium heat. You don't want to brown or crisp them at all. just warm them on both side so that they are pilable.
  6. Keep tortillas warm in foil. These can be palced in a warm oven if needed.
